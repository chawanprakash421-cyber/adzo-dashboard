How to follow up with a lead the right way
5 steps
▼
1
Go to All Leads in the left menu. Find the lead you want to follow up with.
2

3

4
If they don't reply in 24 hours, go to Follow-up Needed — Priya will remind you automatically.
5
After calling, update the lead status to Site Visit Booked or Not Interested so your data stays clean.
📢
How to create your first Google Ad campaign
6 steps
▼
1
Go to Campaigns in the left menu. Click the purple + Create Campaign button.
2
Choose Google Search as the platform. This shows your ad when someone types "2BHK Gachibowli" on Google.
3
Type your property details — location, price, BHK type. Priya will write the ad copy for you automatically.
4
Set your daily budget. We recommend ₹500–₹1,000/day to start. You can always increase later.
5
Review the ad preview, then click Launch Campaign. Your ad goes live within 30 minutes.
6
Check your Analytics after 3 days to see how many people clicked your ad and enquired.

Your wallet is topped up instantly. Your ads continue running without any break.
